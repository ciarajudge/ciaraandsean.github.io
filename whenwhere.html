Do I have to RSVP? Sure you know I'm coming!

No we do not know you're coming! Even if it seems super obvious to you that you'll be there, we are coordinating more than 150 guests and we will assume you are not coming if you have not RSVP'd. This is essential so that we can pass on dietary requirements, allergies and necessary mobility accommodations to the hotel. The RSVP list is also required for the hotel to know how many meals to cook! No RSVP = no meal. Trust us - we made our own parents and siblings RSVP.
